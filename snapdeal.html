Snapdeal brings together a wide assortment of good quality and value- priced merchandise on its platform. Snapdeal's vision is to enable the shoppers of Bharat experience the joy of living their aspirations through reliable, value-for-money shopping. With a personalized, multilingual interface and cutting edge technology, Snapdeal has simplified the shopping experience for its value-conscious buyers by showcasing the most relevant products- products that are a good functional fit with their needs and of a quality that lasts- thereby delivering true value to its customers.

Further, Snapdeal's mission is to become India’s value lifestyle omni-channel leader. We are excited about continuing to build a complete ecosystem around value commerce, where we can serve 'Bharat' consumers wherever they are on their offline to online shopping journey.
